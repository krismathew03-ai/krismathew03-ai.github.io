01 / About

Why this page exists.

This page is hand-written HTML with its own CSS file. No Bootstrap, no templates.

I built it in collaboration with an AI and then shaped the design, content, and structure into something that felt like mine. The layout, the photos, the tone, and most of the decisions came from me. The AI handled the grunt work of turning those decisions into clean code.

I'm Kris Tony, a finance major at BYU. I shoot on a Fujifilm X-T50, design pitch decks for startups on the side, and play too much Rocket League.

02 / Field notes

What I shoot.

A loose taxonomy of what ends up in my drive:

  1. Cities. The obvious playground.
    • neon reflections after rain
    • strangers mid-thought at crosswalks
    • the ten-minute window before golden hour
  2. Portraits. Harder, more rewarding.
    • window light over softbox
    • 56mm prime when possible
    • talk for five minutes before the first frame
  3. Everything else. The overflow folder.
    • basketball games on the block
    • canyon hikes in southern Utah
    • half-finished startup offices
