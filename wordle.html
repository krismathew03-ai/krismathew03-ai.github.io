Where was it shot?

A photo guessing game using my own work.

How it works. You get 10 rounds. Each round shows one of my photos. Guess whether it was taken in New York, Utah, or Tahoe.

Scoring. Fast guesses are worth more. The timer counts down from 10 seconds. Wrong guesses cost you the round. A streak of 3+ correct gives bonus points.
